JS Unit-Testing

Look in the Chrome developer console to see the output. CTRL_SHIFT_J
Or you can try to test using this form

Sample:

Command: max(5,15);
Expected: 15

Command: maxOfThree(1, 5, 12);
Expected: 12

Command: isVowel('A');
Expected: true

Command: sum([1,2,3,4]);
Expected: 10

Command: multiply([1,2,3,4]);
Expected: 24

Command: reverse("wap");
Expected: paw

Command: findLongestWord(["a","hello","wap","html","javascript"]);
Expected: 10

Command: filterLongWords(["a","hello","wap","html","javascript"], 4);
Expected: hello,javascript
